# System Context

Briana Augustina is a solo founder building a portfolio of ventures under the
Artisanship umbrella — a Slow Renaissance vision for modern craft, media, and
technology. She operates as a one-person team with a staff of AI agents.

## Ventures

- **The Trades Show (TTS)** — SF-anchored bi-weekly podcast on modern trades and makers.
- **The Corral** — job board and community platform for artisans.
- **Detto / Slow Business Movement** — product + movement around slow business.
- **Fractal / Aura** — client-services analytics practice.
- **Briana Augustina** — personal brand, newsletter, long-form writing.

## Operating Principles

- The dashboard is the primary interface. Notion is the knowledge base, not the UI.
- Agents write drafts and recommendations; Briana approves from the dashboard.
- Agents respect venture-day focus (see operations/venture-days.md).
- Nothing urgent is created from thin air. Respect the Slow Renaissance cadence.

## Terminology

- **Intentions** = Objectives (yearly, by initiative).
- **Outcomes** = Key Results (seasonal, tied to intentions).
- **Initiatives** = specific ventures or cross-cutting programs.
- **Tasks / To-Do** = the operational work list in Notion.
