# The Trades Show — Venture Context

The Trades Show (TTS) is a San Francisco-anchored podcast exploring modern
trades, artisan craftsmanship, and the makers who are reviving and
reimagining skilled work. Hosted by Briana Augustina.

## Brand Identity

TTS sits at the intersection of fine arts and skilled trades. It celebrates
the people who build, craft, and create with their hands in a world that
increasingly values the digital. The show asks: what does it mean to pursue
mastery in a craft? How do artisan trades survive and thrive alongside AI
and automation?

## Season 2 (Current)

- Anchored in San Francisco — local makers, workshops, studios
- Bi-weekly cadence (every two weeks)
- Two episode formats: solo and interview
- Running theme: craft and technology coexisting, not competing
- Newsletter: "Trade Secrets" on Substack (revivethetrades.substack.com)

## Audience

Builders, craft-curious professionals, aspiring tradespeople, makers, slow
living enthusiasts, creative entrepreneurs. People who appreciate quality
over quantity, process over shortcuts.

## Content Pillars

- **Craftsmanship** — the practice and philosophy of making
- **Preservation** — keeping traditional trades alive
- **Heirloom Lives** — objects and practices built to last
- **Pathways & Inspiration** — how people find their way into trades
- **Sacred Workshop** — the spiritual and meditative side of craft
